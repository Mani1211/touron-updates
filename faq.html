1. What is Planned Tour ?
This tour is perfect for all busy-bee travel enthusiasts! Our itinerary completely depends on you and your preferences and we personalise the whole tour accordingly. We offer you a complete list of things to do, places to visit, etc. and further prepare an appropriate itinerary for you within your budget and according to your travel preferences, making the experience worth every penny!
2. What is Surprise Tour
We surprise you with the best suitable location within your budget and according to your travel preferences. Destination will be revealed only 3 days before your departure date. We don’t have concrete itineraries as we believe that you should decide where you want to invest your money. 3. What is Road Trip ?
It is all about the journey and not the destination.” If this is your mantra, a road trip is the best option for you! A road trip lets you experience the scenic beauty of the places you go by unlike taking a train or a flight. We provide you with appropriate route plans and recommendations of restaurants, fuel stations, etc. We do plan it all out for you, but the decision of what to explore and what not still remains with you.
4. What if I know where I want to go & can I select Surprise Tour ?
No, in this case you have select the Planned Tour Option. If you know you want to go to Kashmir, you should go to Kashmir! But we cannot guarantee that we'll send you there. In fact, we can guarantee we won't send you there, because it wouldn't be a surprise. Surprise Tour is all about embracing the unknown, so if you have a specific trip in mind, we would recommend you our Planned Tour option.
